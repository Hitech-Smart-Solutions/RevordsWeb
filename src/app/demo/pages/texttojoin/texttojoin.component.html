Text-to-Join Codes

Text-to-Join allows your customers to text a simple keyword and instantly join your loyalty program. This is an easy way to get new customers to sign up, no matter where they are!

How does it work?
  • You get a unique code for each of your business groups.
  • Customers text your code to 578-277 (REVORDS).
  • Customers get a confirmation and are automatically opted in to receive your messages going forward.
IMPORTANT LEGAL NOTICE
When you promote your Text-to-Join code, you must always make it clear that customers will be joining your loyalty program, and agree to receive promotional texts from your business.

Here is an example of an appropriate message: "Text [YOUR CODE] to 578-277 to receive ongoing offers from our rewards program!"

In addition you must always include the following legal disclosures wherever you advertise your Text-to-Join code: Standard message and data rates may apply. Text STOP to cancel or HELP for help. Go to revords.com/terms for terms and privacy.
